About

I am a junior backend developer living in Belgium. In 2023 I completed a hands-on training for web developers with the BeCode organization. I chose the backend track because I like the logic of making things work behind the scenes - storing data, building connections, sharing information.


My name is Italian, but I have heard it pronounced in so many ways by now. Over the past ten years I have lived in several different countries, which meant learning new things (and new words!) almost every day. That is when being open to communicate with people and ready to adapt came in handy.


My current goal is to keep diving deep into backend languages and frameworks, and move on to software development. I wish to contribute to building solutions for a fairer world, bit by bit.
